My Favorite Meal (Noodles)

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings. Noodles are a staple food in many cultures.
While long, thin strips may be the most common, many varieties of noodles are cut into waves, helices, tubes, strings, or shells, or folded over, or cut into other shapes. Noodles are usually cooked in boiling water, sometimes with cooking oil or salt added. They are often pan-fried or deep-fried. Noodles are often served with an accompanying sauce or in a soup.

Ingredients needed to make noodles

Ingredient Measurement
Water 1 cup
Noodles 250g
Diced carrot 10g
Onions 20g
Seasoning 1tbsp
Chilli pepper 1tbsp

Steps in making Noodles

  • Pour a cup of water into a pot and allow to boil
  • Add the 250g of noodles and allow to boil for 3 minutes
  • Add the seasoning, Chilli pepper, diced carrot and onions
  • Allow the water to dry and Serve
